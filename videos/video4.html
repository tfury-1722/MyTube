Core Fitness



Ten minutes a day is all you need to strenthen and maintain a healthy core. Exercises like crunches, bicycle, planks and variations of the plank can significantly transform your abdominals. Targeted stress on that particular section of the body can increase metabolic rate which in turn can increase efficient fat burning. Proper form and technique engages the right core muscles and helps to sculpt the stomach area as you one desires.
