Upper Body




Mastering the pull-up exercise is important for developing muscles. There's an ideal range of motion for pull-ups but avoiding hyperextending the elbows is recomended. There is also an ideal rhythmic tempo. Usually there is a two second count upward and a one secound count downward. Slow and controlled movements are emphasized for building strong muscles. Focus on the basics and gradually progress to more advanced variations.
